Our Service Philosophy

Integral to our mission is our guarantee to provide exceptional service for each and every client. In order to make sure we can commit to this, we only select clients that appreciate the time and care that we provide to each and every clean.

This allows us to maintain quality control by keeping our team small, ensuring we can check each job and take the time to provide flawless finishes every time. We don't want the biggest cleaning business with a massive client base. We don't want our clients to feel like just another house to get in & get the job done so we can move on to the next ASAP.

Instead, we consider ourselves personal assistants to each & every client who takes the time to listen and implement different requests and even takes the time to go above and beyond the set tasks to complete items not listed if they need doing.

We want our clients to come home and go WOW! To smell, feel & experience the Deep Clean difference.

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

We're not satisfied until you are. If you're not completely happy with our service, we'll return within 24 hours to make it right at no additional charge.
