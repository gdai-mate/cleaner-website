Heritage Queenslander Revival - West End

West End, Brisbane Deep Clean & Restoration 2 Days

The Challenge

This beautiful 1920s Queenslander had been neglected for several months. The humid Brisbane climate had taken its toll, with mould growth in bathrooms, dust accumulation on intricate period features, and deteriorated hardwood floors throughout.

Our Approach

  • Gentle cleaning of heritage timber features using specialized products
  • Professional mould remediation in high-humidity areas
  • Restoration cleaning of original hardwood floors
  • Detailed cleaning of period light fixtures and ceiling roses
  • Window restoration including heritage sash windows

Results Achieved

The home was transformed from a tired, neglected property to a stunning showcase of Brisbane's architectural heritage. Original timber features now gleam, floors have been restored to their former glory, and the entire home feels fresh and revitalized.

"I couldn't believe it was the same house. DEEP CLEAN understood how to care for the heritage features while bringing everything back to life. The attention to detail was incredible."

Post-Construction Clean - Birkdale New Build

Birkdale, Brisbane Bayside 3-Phase Construction Clean 3 Days

The Challenge

A newly constructed 4-bedroom home in Birkdale required comprehensive post-construction cleaning. Construction dust had settled throughout, windows were covered in residue, and the coastal location meant salt air exposure during the build process.

Our 3-Phase Process

  • Phase 1: Initial dust and debris removal from all surfaces
  • Phase 2: Detailed cleaning of all fixtures, fittings, and surfaces
  • Phase 3: Final polish including floor sealing and window treatment
  • Specialized attention to coastal environment challenges
  • Premium floor protection and final clean before handover

Results Achieved

The home was delivered in pristine, move-in condition. Every surface sparkled, all construction residue was completely removed, and the new owners received their dream home exactly as intended by the architect's vision.

"The builder recommended DEEP CLEAN and we're so glad they did. The house was absolutely perfect when we got the keys - not a speck of dust anywhere."

- Michael & Lisa T., Birkdale

Riverfront Apartment Deep Clean - Kangaroo Point

Kangaroo Point, Brisbane Humidity Recovery Clean 1 Day

The Challenge

This modern riverfront apartment had been vacant for 6 months during summer. The combination of Brisbane's humidity and river proximity had created mould issues, while floor-to-ceiling windows showed significant water marking from storms.

Our Specialized Approach

  • Professional mould treatment using hospital-grade products
  • Specialized river-view window cleaning (12 floors up)
  • HVAC system deep clean and filter replacement
  • Humidity damage assessment and treatment
  • Balcony deep clean including glass balustrade treatment

Results Achieved

The apartment was completely restored to its modern, pristine condition. All mould issues were eliminated, windows provided crystal-clear river views, and the space felt fresh and welcoming for new tenants.
