Meet the DEEP CLEAN Team

Our Story

DEEP CLEAN is a locally owned boutique cleaning service located in the Bayside of Brisbane. Founded by Amie Eckhardt, we've built our reputation on a simple principle: quality over quantity.

We specialise in high-end residential and post-construction cleaning, but what truly sets us apart is our approach. We deliberately keep our team small and our client base selective. Why? Because we believe you deserve more than just another cleaning service rushing through your home.

We consider ourselves personal assistants to each client - taking time to listen, implement special requests, and often going above and beyond the set tasks. When we notice something that needs attention, we take care of it. No automated responses, no rushed jobs, no treating your home like just another house on the list.

Our commitment to exceptional service means we only partner with clients who appreciate the time and care we invest in every clean. This allows us to maintain strict quality control, personally check each job, and ensure those flawless finishes that have become our signature.

We want you to come home and go "WOW!" - to smell, feel, and experience the Deep Clean difference.
